Xtracycle Ride to the Dead Baby Bikes' Bat Cave for a Concert and Party!

Friday November 10th

Kipchoge Spencer co-owner of Xtracycle and lead singer of the Ginger Ninjas
Opening Band: Skwish (Dead Baby Terry is the Drummer!) Skwish has to cancel because one of the band members is out of town.

We will have 2 kegs of Pacific Rim Brewing beer. IPA and Stout.

Bicycling Season Opening Day Ride: January 1, 2008

Join us NYD 2008 at 12 Noon at Greenlake. Meet near the basketball court, near the baseball fields, near the restrooms at the east side of the lake.

LOCATION

While there will be plenty of cargo bikes, this ride is for anyone.

Cargo travel on the slow side (12 MPH). We try to stay together, but sometimes we don't make it to the proposed location.

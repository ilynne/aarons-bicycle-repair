Victor Falls, The Seattle area's biggest mountain bike Mecca. The trails have everything from beginner to expert. There are literally hundreds of miles of trails and fire roads. The place is so big, you will hardly see any other riders even on a crowded day. We got back to the truck and the parking lot was full, but the only sign of other bikers were their tire tracks. Lots of mud! Your bike will be very, very dirty. Dumonde Tech chain lube works great here. We often try to ID the various tire tracks, WTB Velociraptor, Ritchey Z-Max, Tioga and others are often seen. Big tires are better here. There is a lot of new bridge construction going on here. Some of it crazy. Most well built and easy to ride.

If you want a map stop by Bonney Lake Bicycle Shop and pick one up for $2. Well worth it, otherwise your little ride could turn into an epic night-time where the heck are we ride! For directions, the pages below have good descriptions. One change is the name of the Christmas tree farm, It is now called Hillside Farm Xmas Trees, Choose and Cut. Please buy your tree from these nice people. They let us park there.

Ah! Skookum Flats! Always one heck of a trail. The forest service is doing a great job of maintaining this trail. Please pay for your pass. We started this ride at the north end and rode to the first road and back, about 12 miles.
Chad works for Browning Transmissions and rode a fully functional 12 speed system. It rocks and had no problems! You can even shift the front gears standing on the steepest sections. WOW!

Aaron's bike is a demo and if you want to try it out, then we will loan it to you for FREE! We also have a Pro~Flex 857 you can ride. The catch is you must be going on one of our shop rides, otherwise you can rent either bike for $45 per day.

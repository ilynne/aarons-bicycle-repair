BIKE TO WORK DAY MAY 18, 2007


We always bring everything on cargo bikes!

We always have our traditional "RideYourBike.com" organic bananas from West Seattle Thriftway and free coffee from Starbucks.

Enviro-Note: With the exception of some wrappers 100% of the trash was recycled from our station! We used reuseable ceramic cups purchased at the Goodwill for 29 cents each, which we wash and re-use every year. All the banana peels were composted!

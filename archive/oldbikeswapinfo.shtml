UPCOMMING BIKE SWAP MEETS:

5th Annual Tacoma Bike Swap

SATURDAY, APRIL 27th 10AM-2PM
University of Puget Sound Memorial Fieldhouse, N 11th Ave and Union, Tacoma, WA
Want to sell one of those bikes cluttering up the garage? Looking for that perfect vintage cruiser? Whether buying or selling, join us for this exciting community event! While you’re there, check out these highlights:
Free Bike Corral for anyone with just one or two bikes to sell—with a new section this year just for kids bikes, scoots, and trailers!
Low-cost adult and children’s helmets and free helmet fittings with Downtown on the Go!
Bike experts from GeoEngineers to offer advice at the Bike Corral and do minor bike repairs.
Free flat tire repairs with Tacoma Bike
Children’s Bike Roadeo.
Hosted by University of Puget Sound

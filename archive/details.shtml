Remodeling Floor Plan


We have expanded the shop!




The old back wall existed between the 2 repair benches. The area above it was the old shop's size. As you can see we gained a lot more space. About 4 times as much! We are now selling complete bikes and more parts and accessories. Not only more selection but more items like : shoes, helmets & clothing.

We are carrying Raleigh and Marin Bicycles plus BMX brands Torker, Haro.












The Old Front Porch is a place to read the latest bike literature, surf the web, wait for your bike to be repaired, or just hang-out. Everyone is welcome to use the FREE public internet access! There is a nice comfy sofa, a VCR with bike videos, FREE STARBUCKS COFFEE and tea and the famous ABR Library were you can check out books just like the public library. We have over 100 titles.

If you don't know what an Xtracycle is then click here or go to Xtracycle.com

Here is our planned Route. About 9 Miles with a stop for lunch at The Ram Restaurant & Brewery in University Village.

This ride was not just for Xtracyclers. All bikers, cyclists, riders and passengers are invited! This ride is all about the usefullness of the bicycle. Cargo hauling is THE key feature of the Xtracycle and since it is still a bicycle it is very fun to ride!

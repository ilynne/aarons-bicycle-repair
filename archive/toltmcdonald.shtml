Tolt McDonald Park is easy to get to and has great trails. The 3/4 climb will be the only obstacle you face. The trails are well groomed and twisty, but very muddy in winter. This place is really fun! We used the map on the right and got lost. No big deal because it is mostly rolling and flat on top, down was to the truck! We eventually came out on a road and found our way again. Getting lost is part of the fun of mountain biking as long as you are not too far from civilization. Jason is investing in a compass so we can find and ride more trails.

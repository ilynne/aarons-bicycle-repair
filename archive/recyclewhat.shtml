What can be recycled in our city Mixed Recycling Container ?
Paper YES NO

• Advertising mail
• Catalogs
• Cardboard (flatten, place in or next to cart)
• Cereal boxes (remove liners)
• Clean paper egg cartons
• Computer paper
• Envelopes (plastic window is ok)
• Magazines
• Newspapers
• Notebook paper
• Paperback books
• Phone books
• Stationery
• Tablet backs
• Wrapping paper and cards
• Waxed juice boxes (please rinse)
• Waxed milk cartons (please rinse)

• Paper soiled with food waste
• Food soiled material
• Paper towels, napkins, tissues or paper plates
• Thermal fax paper
Plastics YES NO
(clean and empty)
• Bottles, jugs and jars
(all colors, rinsed, no lids)

 - Water, juice and cola (liter) bottles;
 - Medicine and shampoo bottles
 - Peanut butter jars
• Round food tubs (all colors, rinsed, no lids)
 - Yogurt, salsa, and cottage cheese containers
 - Cool-whip or other frozen food containers
• Plastic shopping bags (clean, dry, tied in a bag)
• Merchandise or department store bags (clean, dry, tied in a bag; no receipts, twist ties, plastic fasteners, or strings)
• Dry cleaning bags (clean, dry, tied in a bag)
• Newspaper bags		 • Plastic bottles that have contained motor oil, pesticides, other hazardous materials
• Plastic film wrap
• Lids or caps
• Plastic toys
• Plastic take-out containers or utensils
• Plastic trays, fast food containers, clam shell containers
• Disposable plastic cups
• Loose or food-contaminated plastic bags
• Sandwich-type bags
• Film canisters 
• Prescription pill vials
• Disposable diapers
• Nursery/flower pots
• Foam
• Styrofoam

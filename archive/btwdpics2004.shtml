You can make any day bike to work day! Let us show you how or sign up for the Bike Buddy Program and get a mentor to show you the ropes.
We had our traditional 'RideYourBike.com' Organic Bananas from West Seattle Thriftway and many other goodies from Cascade Bicycle Club. The day started out grey but a warm 55 degrees F. For those who drove, see you next year!

Enviro-Note: With the exception of some wrappers 100% of the trash was recycled from our station! We used reuseable ceramic cups purchased at the Goodwill for 29 cents each and will wash and save them for next year. All the banana peels were composted by Aaron & Gypsie's chickens. Starbucks fell through on the coffee this year, we will hit up someone else next year, perhaps Uptown or Cafe Ladro or Java Bean. Does anyone know those folks and can ask? Call Gypsie if you do.

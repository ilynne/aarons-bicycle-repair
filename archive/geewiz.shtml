Gypsie Wanders West Seattle
(Gee-Wiz)

This ride has been post-poned untill the spring of 2002

Most Saturday mornings Gypsie will lead an easy road ride around West Seattle. We leave promptly at 9:00 AM from the shop. The pace is kept easy and the length is less than 15 miles. This is a very good ride for beginners or those just wanting to enjoy the morning and some good comradeship. There is usually one regroup/rest stop to enjoy the scenery......or grab a latte!

This is a Cascade Bicycle Club ride. Helmets are required!

Please call (206) 938-9795 or email (gypsie@rideyourbike.com) to R.S.V.P.

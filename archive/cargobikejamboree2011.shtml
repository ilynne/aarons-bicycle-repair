Meet near the Pike Place Pig (she should be back from the hospital) and ride to Lincoln Park in West Seattle, a totally flat ride! (Well, almost flat, just some very tiny short easy hills like the lower West Seattle Bridge).

This is a stick-together ride and there will be one refueling/supplies stop at 7-11 along Harbor Avenue for supplies and ice for the bike blended smoothies! This is our approximate route , but subject to the group's whim.

If you want to meet us there, we will be at shelter #3 on the south beach around 3 PM.

Look for Xtracycles, Trailers, Front Racks, Rear Racks, Panniers, Bakfiets, Porter Bikes, Messenger Bags, etc., etc.

Tool Sharing

The place for Home Owners and Friends to find tools to borrow.
Why buy when you can borrow?

Rules:

  • Just because a tool is listed, doesn't mean it is available to you!
  • The loaning of tools is agreed upon by the individual parties.
  • Please return tools with-in 2 weeks
  • All tools are to be returned in BETTER CONDITION than when borrowed.
    This means Cleaned, Lubed and Repaired.
  • Mark all your tools with your name!
  • If you break someone's tool, please replace it with a new one.

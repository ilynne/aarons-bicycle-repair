A 24 hole Rim Laced to a 36 hole Hub

This was done because a 36 hole rim in the 16 x 1 3/8" (ISO 349mm) size is very rare.
This is from a Bike Friday Tikit.

The drive side spokes are 128mm (.833 cross). There are 2 spoke lengths for the non-driveside 145mm (1.667 cross) and 152mm( 2 cross).
The ERD for this rim is 338mm
We used this online spoke lenght calculator: http://lenni.info/edd

Special thanks to Chalo Colina for his drawings and help designing this wheel.

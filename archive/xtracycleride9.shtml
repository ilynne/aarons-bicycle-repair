A Family Haulin' 4th of July!

Independence Day 2007 at Noon

Meet at the Pergola at First and Yesler in Pioneer Square at Noon. We leave by 12:30!

However, if you are late, we will be stopping by Uwajimaya for supplies so, meet us there if you are late.

Our destination is Terminal 107 Park along the Duwamish River. For those of you that have not been to this park, let me just say, it is unknown and very nice!

Easter: the day the bunnies hatch!

Springtime is very much here! Come celebrate life and ride with Val Kleitz. "I ain't dead yet!" so says he!

Meet at High Noon on April 4th near the METAL ROCK, at the north end of the Delridge Playfield; near the Delridge Community Center, Google Map View (you can see the rock in the Google view). The Playfield is across the street from the Youngstown Cultural Arts Center.

Depart at 1 PM SHARP! Destination: Seward Park

CARGO BIKE NOT REQUIRED:
Cargo bike rides are always family oriented. Bring your own plates, utensils, cups. Leave no trace! While there will be plenty of cargo bikes, this ride is for everyone. Cargo bikes travel SLOWLY! (8-10 MPH) and we stay together.

Mountain Biking on Vashon Island

The closest mountain bike trails to Seattle are just 5 miles down the main highway on Vashon Island. Take the Fauntleroy Ferry. You can also catch a bus at the ferry dock on a weekday or Saturday (no bus service on Sunday). If you ride, the hardest part is just after the ferry dock for 1 mile. After that it is 4 more miles to the quiet town of Vashon. Stop by Vashon Island Bicycles for a free map of the trails or print out this one. These trails are fun and offer something for everyone. However, if you are into more aggressive, 'North Shore Style' riding, go to Dockton.

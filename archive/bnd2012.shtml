All bikes welcome.
Fork the mall and shopping! Busting doors is no fun, it's Buy Nothing Day! Come ride your bike!
Let's burn off some calories from the national Day of Feasting while avoiding the crazed hordes of shopping zombies.

Bring your donations for the Val Kleitz Memorial Bronze Statue Fund! Or just drink a beer in his memory.

Meet at Noon at the Pike Place Pig

Seattle Cargo Bike Ride Custom dictates, we meet at Noon, leave at 1 PM.

We will ride to the Hat and Boots (a.k.a. Oxbow Park).

It is usually raining and cold, so bring warm clothing, charcoal & FIRE! BBQ grills, hot beverages, leftovers from T-day.

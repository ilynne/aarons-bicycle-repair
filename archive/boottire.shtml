How to boot a bicycle tire.

  • Remove and dry tire.
  • Turn tire inside out at hole area.
  • Use Super Glue to seal the cut or hole.

  • Cut a piece of old tire sidewall into a circle or oval to cover cut area.
    You can get an old tire for FREE at any bike shop!
    We have pre-cut sections for FREE also.

  • Fix the boot in place with duct tape.
    Some adhesives like contact cement or Barge cement also work.
